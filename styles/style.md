STYLE CONTRACT — Causal Discovery Deck
=======================================
Theme: "Midnight Executive"
  - Primary (navy):    #1E2761
  - Secondary (ice):   #CADCFC
  - White:             #FFFFFF
  - Body background:   #F4F7FF
  - Muted gray:        #8899BB
  - Body text dark:    #2D3B6E
  - Card text:         #3D4F7C

Fonts:
  - Header font:  Georgia, 30–48pt bold (slide titles 30pt, main title 48pt)
  - Body font:    Calibri, 13–15pt
  - Captions:     Calibri, 10–12pt italic
  - Slide number: Calibri, 10pt, muted gray, bottom-right

Slide size: 13.3" × 7.5" (LAYOUT_WIDE, 16:9)

Logo/branding: none

Layout patterns:
  - Title slide:       Full navy background, large Georgia title, ice blue vertical accent bar left of title, mini DAG illustration bottom-right, large faint circle top-right
  - Section divider:   Full navy background, large bold section number (96pt), horizontal rule, section title (40pt), subtitle tag line below
  - Content slide:     Light body background (#F4F7FF), navy header bar (full width, h=1.05"), content below at y=1.3"
  - Two-column:        Bullet text left (w≈5.8"), white card with illustration right (w≈5.4")
  - Card grid:         White cards with coloured top stripe, ice blue badge tag, description text
  - Stat callouts:     Navy rectangle blocks, large stat value (Georgia 36pt ice blue), divider rule, label below

Motif: DAG-inspired network nodes
  - Faint connected circles + lines, top-right corner of every slide
  - Dark slides: ice blue nodes, ~18% opacity
  - Light slides: navy nodes, ~10% opacity

Section divider colors: navy full-bleed (#1E2761) with white title

Spacing:
  - Slide margins: 0.55" left, 0.5" right
  - Header bar: x=0, y=0, w=13.3", h=1.05"
  - Content starts at y=1.3" on content slides
  - Card padding: 0.12–0.15" internal margin
  - Element gaps: 0.3–0.5" between blocks
